Salary Breakdown

Understanding your salary is key to managing your finances effectively. Here are the main components:

  • Basic Pay: The core salary amount before any additions or deductions.
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA): Portion of your salary given to cover accommodation costs.
  • Allowances: Other benefits like conveyance, medical, special allowances, etc.
  • Provident Fund (PF): A retirement savings fund deducted monthly.
  • Tax: Income tax deducted based on your salary.

Doughnut chart showing salary distribution: Basic Pay 50%, HRA 20%, Allowances 15%, PF 10%, Tax 5%.

Tax Info

Tax Slabs

  • Income up to ₹2.5 lakh: No tax
  • ₹2.5 lakh - ₹5 lakh: 5%
  • ₹5 lakh - ₹10 lakh: 20%
  • Above ₹10 lakh: 30%

Deductions

  • Section 80C: Up to ₹1.5 lakh (Investments, PPF, ELSS)
  • Standard Deduction: ₹50,000
  • Health Insurance under 80D
  • Home Loan Interest under 24(b)

Tax-Saving Options

  • Invest in PPF, ELSS, NSC
  • Use House Rent Allowance (HRA) exemption
  • Utilize Health and Education loan benefits
  • Claim deductions on insurance premiums

Financial Education

  • Budgeting: Track income & expenses to control cash flow effectively.
  • Emergency Fund: Save at least 3-6 months of expenses for unexpected events.
  • Compounding: Let your investments grow by reinvesting earnings over time.
  • Investments: Diversify between stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and real estate for growth and security.
